# Eolas Medical — Context

## Overview
AI-powered knowledge retrieval platform for healthcare. Transforms institutional medical content (guidelines, protocols, educational materials) into searchable, accessible information at point of care. Named from Irish word for "knowledge" (pronounced "oh-lus").

**Stage:** Series A ($12M raised), 400+ teams, 360k+ healthcare professionals
**HQ:** Ireland (Declan based there)
**Primary Markets:** UK (NHS trusts), Ireland (HSE), expanding to US
